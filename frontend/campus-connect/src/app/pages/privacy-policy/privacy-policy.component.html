Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains what information CampusConnect collects and stores, how it is used, and who can access it, especially for the chat feature. It is designed to align with FERPA.

1) What CampusConnect Stores

A) Account and Housing Information

CampusConnect may store:

  • Name, university email, university ID number (or unique user identifier)
  • Role (Resident, RA, Housing Staff or Administrator)
  • Housing assignment details (building or community, room or unit, move in and move out dates)
  • Notification preferences and communication settings

B) Chat Content and Attachments

CampusConnect stores:

  • Messages you send and receive (including edits, reactions, and replies)
  • Attachments (images or files) you upload in chat
  • Channel membership (for example floor, building, staff channels)

C) Chat Metadata and Activity Logs

CampusConnect stores:

  • Timestamps, recipients or channel IDs, delivery status, and read indicators (if enabled)
  • Reports you submit (for example report message, block or mute actions)
  • Moderation and audit logs (for example who accessed a record and when)

D) Device and Technical Data

CampusConnect may store:

  • Device identifiers (for example device type and OS, app version)
  • Push notification token
  • IP address and basic log data used for security and troubleshooting

2) How We Use Stored Data

We use stored data to:

  • Provide chat, notifications, and housing communications
  • Support resident life operations (community updates, RA outreach, coordination)
  • Investigate reports, policy violations, and safety concerns
  • Maintain system security, prevent fraud or spam, and troubleshoot issues
  • Meet institutional, legal, and record retention obligations

3) Who Can View Chat Data (Including “Deleted” Messages)

A) Authorized Housing Personnel Access

CampusConnect is a university housing system. Resident Advisors (RAs) and Housing administrators may access chat history when they have a legitimate educational interest, such as:

  • Responding to resident reports or safety concerns
  • Addressing harassment, threats, or policy violations
  • Supporting conduct or housing contract processes
  • Managing official housing communications channels

Access is limited to authorized personnel, is role based, and may be logged and audited.

B) “Delete” Does Not Mean Erase From University Records

If you delete a chat message in CampusConnect:

  • It may be removed from your view (and or marked as deleted in the conversation), but it may still be retained in:
  • system logs
  • backups
  • audit records
  • or administrative tools used for safety, compliance, and investigations

RAs and administrators can view and retrieve chat history even if a user deletes messages, when permitted by university policy and FERPA.

4) FERPA and “Education Records”

Some CampusConnect data, including certain chat communications, may qualify as an education record if it is directly related to a student and maintained by the university (or a party acting for the university).

When CampusConnect data is treated as part of a student’s education record, FERPA governs how it may be accessed and disclosed.

5) Sharing Data Outside Housing

We do not sell student data.

We may share information only as allowed by university policy and applicable law, including FERPA, such as:

  • With other university offices (for example Student Conduct, Title IX, IT Security, Dean of Students) when needed for official purposes
  • With vendors or service providers that help operate CampusConnect (for example hosting, customer support) only under contract and only to perform university functions, consistent with FERPA requirements for school officials
  • With appropriate parties in a health or safety emergency when necessary to protect individuals
  • When required by law (for example lawful court orders or subpoenas) under established university procedures

6) Data Retention

CampusConnect retains data according to the university’s records retention schedule and operational needs. Retention may be extended due to:

  • investigations
  • conduct or housing proceedings
  • litigation holds
  • or security incidents

Backups may persist for a period of time even after content is no longer visible in the app.

7) Security

We use reasonable safeguards designed to protect CampusConnect data, such as:

  • access controls based on role
  • logging and auditing of administrative access
  • secure hosting practices
  • security monitoring

No system can be guaranteed 100% secure.

8) Your FERPA Rights

Depending on your status as an eligible student, FERPA generally provides rights to:

  • request to inspect and review your education records
  • request amendment of records you believe are inaccurate or misleading
  • have some control over disclosures of education records, with exceptions (such as disclosures to school officials with legitimate educational interest)
  • file a complaint with the U.S. Department of Education’s Student Privacy Policy Office
