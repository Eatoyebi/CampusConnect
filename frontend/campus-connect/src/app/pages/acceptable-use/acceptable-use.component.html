Campus Connect — University Housing Chat Acceptable Use Policy (AUP)

1) Purpose

The Campus Connect chat feature exists to support a safe, respectful residential community by enabling residents, Resident Advisors (RAs), and Housing staff to communicate about housing-related topics (e.g., community updates, roommate coordination, facility questions, event info, and general support).

2) Scope

This policy applies to all users of the Campus Connect chat function, including residents, approved guests with access, RAs, and Housing staff. Use of chat indicates agreement to follow this policy and all applicable university policies (e.g., Student Code of Conduct, anti-discrimination/harassment policies, and IT/security policies).

3) Expectations for Chat Behavior

Users must:

  • Be respectful and civil in tone and content.
  • Communicate in good faith (avoid escalation, clarify misunderstandings).
  • Keep messages housing-relevant in community and staff channels.
  • Use appropriate language (no slurs or degrading remarks).
  • Follow RA/Housing staff direction in official channels.

4) Prohibited Use

The following are not allowed in Campus Connect chat:

A) Harassment, Discrimination, and Harmful Conduct

  • Bullying, intimidation, harassment, stalking, or targeted hostility
  • Discriminatory or hateful content (including slurs) toward protected groups
  • Sexual harassment or unwanted sexual comments
  • Threats of harm toward others or encouragement of harmful behavior

B) Privacy and Personal Data Misuse

  • Sharing someone’s private information without permission (e.g., phone number, address, ID numbers, schedules, location)
  • Posting screenshots/chat logs outside the app to shame, threaten, or target others
  • Collecting or storing others’ messages for retaliation or coercion

C) Impersonation and Deception

  • Pretending to be another user, RA, staff member, or university office
  • Presenting false information as “official” or intentionally misleading others

D) Unsafe or Illegal Activity

  • Content that promotes or facilitates illegal activity
  • Buying/selling prohibited items or services through chat
  • Encouraging dangerous activities

E) Security and Platform Abuse

  • Phishing, scams, or attempts to obtain passwords/sensitive information
  • Malicious links, malware, or attempts to bypass app security
